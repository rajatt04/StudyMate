# Privacy Policy for StudyMate

**Last Updated:** March 2026

## Introduction

StudyMate ("we", "our", or "the app") is a student management application designed for educational institutions. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, and role (Admin/Faculty/Student)
- **Academic Data**: Class assignments, timetables, grades, and attendance records
- **Notices**: Posted notices and announcements

### Data Stored Locally
All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted database. We do **not** transmit your data to external servers.

## How We Use Your Information

- To authenticate and manage user accounts
- To display academic information relevant to your role
- To facilitate communication between institution members

## Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- Passwords are hashed using PBKDF2WithHmacSHA256 with a unique salt
- Session data is encrypted using Android EncryptedSharedPreferences
- Network communications are restricted to HTTPS only

## Data Sharing

We do **not** share, sell, or transmit your personal data to third parties.

## Children's Privacy

StudyMate does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

## Your Rights

You may:
- Request access to your stored data
- Request deletion of your account
- Export your data

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
